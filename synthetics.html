Periodiska Systemet

Human-Made Chemicals

1. Polymers / Plastics

  • Polyethylene (PE) – plastic bags, bottles
  • Polypropylene (PP) – packaging, automotive parts
  • Polystyrene (PS) – disposable cups, insulation
  • Polyvinyl chloride (PVC) – pipes, vinyl flooring
  • Teflon (PTFE) – non-stick cookware

2. Pharmaceuticals / Drugs

  • Aspirin (acetylsalicylic acid) – pain relief
  • Paracetamol (acetaminophen) – analgesic
  • Penicillin (synthetic derivatives) – antibiotics
  • Statins – cholesterol-lowering drugs
  • Chemotherapy agents (cisplatin, doxorubicin)

3. Dyes & Pigments

  • Mauveine – first synthetic dye (purple)
  • Azo dyes – bright colors in textiles
  • Phthalocyanine blue/green – paints, inks
  • Rhodamine dyes – fluorescent dyes for microscopy

4. Pesticides / Herbicides / Insecticides

  • DDT – banned insecticide
  • Glyphosate – herbicide (Roundup)
  • Malathion / Parathion – insecticides
  • Atrazine – herbicide for crops

5. Industrial Chemicals

  • Ammonia (NH₃) – fertilizers, cleaning agents
  • Sulfuric acid (H₂SO₄) – industrial reactions, batteries
  • Nitric acid (HNO₃) – explosives, fertilizers
  • Ethylene glycol – antifreeze, polyester production
  • Acetone – solvent in paints, plastics

6. Synthetic Fuels & Additives

  • MTBE (methyl tert-butyl ether) – gasoline additive
  • Synthetic diesel – Fischer–Tropsch process
  • Bio-based synthetic fuels – renewable diesel

7. Cosmetics & Personal Care Chemicals

  • Parabens – preservatives in cosmetics
  • Sodium lauryl sulfate – in soaps, shampoos
  • Retinoids – anti-aging creams
  • Synthetic fragrances – vanillin, limonene derivatives

8. Synthetic Food Additives

  • Aspartame – artificial sweetener
  • Tartrazine – yellow food coloring
  • Monosodium glutamate (MSG) – flavor enhancer
  • Sodium benzoate – preservative

9. Advanced Materials / Nanochemicals

  • Carbon nanotubes – electronics, composites
  • Graphene oxide – sensors, coatings
  • Quantum dots – imaging, displays
  • Metal-organic frameworks (MOFs) – gas storage, catalysis
